Adhering to the Law: The Lifeline of Our Community

The Doctrine of Adherence

"Coming to Christ is not a mere religious act—it is an act of Adherence. Just as we adhere to the Governmental Laws of the United States of America for the sake of civic order, we adhere to the Spiritual Laws of Christ for the sake of communal peace. Adhering to civilian leadership is the only way to heal our urban environments. The best people to lead this charge are the ones who have been through it themselves—those who have learned from the struggle and now see through the struggle to the solution."

Our vision is to defend Philadelphia and the entire Commonwealth of Pennsylvania. This defense is our lifeline. We depend on the commitment of our congregation to fund the support systems that our urban centers need most.
