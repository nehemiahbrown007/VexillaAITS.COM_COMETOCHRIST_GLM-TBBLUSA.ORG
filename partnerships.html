YOUNG POLITICIANS™

"Teaching the Children to Write the Law they must Live."

Founded and Trademarked by TBBLUSA.ORG, the Young Politicians™ initiative is our solution for a future of civic peace. We don't wait for our youth to reach the voting booth to teach them politics. We start at the root.

  • Legislative Literacy: Kids learn to draft community ordinances.
  • Ethical Debate: Learning to defend the "Laws of God" in the arena of the "Laws of the State."
  • Public Stewardship: Hands-on interaction with local municipal leaders.

Young Politicians™ is an exclusive trademarked program of the Thin Black and Brown Line USA (TBBLUSA.ORG) ecosystem.
