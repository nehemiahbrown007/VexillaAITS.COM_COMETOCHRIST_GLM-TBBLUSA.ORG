A Soulful Mandate for Community Restoration

"We are here to live, but we are also here to uphold. Our organization keeps its promises because a covenant without works is a breach of both divine and civil duty. We do not force the issue; we present the Evidence of a Better Way. We provide the Solution (the Answer) to the recidivism that plagues our brothers. We are a helping hand with both arms open—welcoming you into a fellowship where the spirit is fed and the civic record is redeemed."

We stand at a critical level of community involvement. Come to Christ is not merely a church; it is a spiritual corporation for community improvement. We have built a spot where corporate giants looking for laborers with heart can find our members. We specialize in the Leadership and Mentoring Skills that keep underprivileged kids in urban environments safe, focused, and prepared for high-level success. This is our testament. This is our helping hand.
